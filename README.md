## Adidas Sales Analysis – Power BI Project
This project presents a complete Power BI dashboard analyzing Adidas sales performance across the United States.
The goal is to provide data-driven insights for business decision regarding product performance, regional trends, profitability and pricing.
## Business Requirements:
•	Gain a deeper understanding of overall sales and profitability patterns.

•	Identify geographical areas with high and low sales potential.

•	Analyze product level performance to support inventory and marketing decisions.

•	Optimize pricing strategies by studying Units sold and price per unit.

•	Provide actionable insights for improving sales and profitability across all dimensions.
## Dataset Summary
The dataset includes details such as Retailer, Product, Category, Region, State, Units Sold, Total Sales, Operating Profit, Price per Unit, and Invoice Date.

## Key KPIs
•	Total Sales

•	Operating Profit

•	Units Sold

•	Price per Unit

•	Operating Margin

## Visualizations Used
•	Area Chart – Monthly Sales Trend

•	Filled Map – Sales by State

•	Donut Chart – Sales by Region

•	Bar Charts – Sales by Product & Retailer

•	KPI Cards – Overall metrics

## Key Insights
•	West and Northeast regions generated the highest revenue.

•	Men's Footwear and Apparel dominated total sales.

•	West Gear and Foot Locker were top forming retailers.

•	Sales peaked in August and December indicating seasonal demand.

•	Some states consistently underperformed, showing opportunities for targeted strategies.


demonstrates effective use of Power BI for end-to-end data analysis and storytelling.
